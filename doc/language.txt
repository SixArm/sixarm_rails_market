!! Languages

We use these languages:
  * DE: Deutsch a.k.a. German
  * EN: English
  * ES: Espanol a.k.a. Spanish
  * FR: French
  * HI: Hindi
  * IT: Italian
  * JA: Japanese
  * ZH: Chinese

We use these naming conventions for strings:
  * name_in_de: Name in Deutsh a.k.a. German
  * name_in_en: Name in English
  * name_in_es: Name in Espanol a.k.a. Spanish
  * etc.
