!! Currencies

We use these currencies:
  * CHF: Swiss Franc
  * CNY: China Yuan
  * EUR: Euro
  * GBP: Great Britain Pound
  * INR: India Rupee
  * JPY: Japan Yen
  * USD: United States Dollar

We use these naming conventions for currencies:
  * price_in_chf: Price in Swiss Francs
  * price_in_cny: Price in China Yuans
  * price_in_eur: Price in Euros
  * etc.
